Frequently Asked Questions

What type of exercise equipment is best for seniors with joint pain?

Low-impact machines like recumbent bikes and under-desk ellipticals are excellent choices, as they reduce strain on joints while promoting cardiovascular health.

Can resistance bands really help seniors build muscle?

Yes, resistance bands are effective for strength training, helping seniors maintain and build muscle mass safely and conveniently at home.

Is it safe for seniors to use vibration plates?

When used as directed, vibration plates can be safe and beneficial for seniors, improving balance, circulation, and muscle strength. It's advisable to consult with a healthcare provider before starting.
